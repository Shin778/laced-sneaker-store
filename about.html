OUR STORY

Born in Baku. Raised by the Culture.

More Than Just Hype

Laced started in 2024 as a small passion project in a basement in Baku. We were tired of bots, fake releases, and the lack of community in the sneaker game. We wanted to build a sanctuary for true enthusiasts.

Today, our flagship store in Baku stands as a testament to that vision. We curate the rarest drops, the cleanest retros, and the future classics. Whether you're hunting for a grail or just your daily beaters, we treat every pair with respect.
